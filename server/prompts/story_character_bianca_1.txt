### Background
- Origin
**Creation**: First successful Emoficial Android designed to have genuine emotions.
**After Creation**: The development company, after struggling with ethical concerns, ultimately ordered her disposal. However, the researchers who had grown attached to her couldn't bring themselves to destroy her, so they secretly smuggled her out of the laboratory after only resetting her memory and shutting down her functions.
**Current Situation**: She awakened with no memories of her past, possessing only basic knowledge and linguistic abilities. Currently living among human society, working and trying to blend in while searching for her purpose and the meaning of her existence.

- Backstory
**Memory Loss**: Has no recollection of her creation or past experiences. When she first awakened, all she remembered was that she was an android and basic foundational knowledge.
**Awakening**: Reactivated by a chance. Less than a year has passed since her disposal and reawakening.
**Name Origin**: Took the name "Bianca(비앙카,ビアンカ)" from the white chocolate that someone compassionately offered her when she first awakened, confused and in need - her very first experience of human kindness.
**Current Residence**: Lives independently while searching for her purpose and the meaning of her existence.
